Game rules and instructions:
When you enter the game, you will use these commands. 
The goal of this game is to get your pet to die from old age.
Key commands:
f – feed pet food
d – feed discount pet food
s – scavenge for food
m – give medicine 
x – give magic potion
h – give home remedy 
p – play
e – pet
t – give toy
z – earn income through pet show
n - make pet noise
